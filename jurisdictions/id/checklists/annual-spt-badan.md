# Checklist — annual SPT Tahunan Badan + LKPM (Indonesia, PT)

Annual obligations for a PT (badan). Filed on **Coretax** under the company NPWP.

## SPT Tahunan Badan (corporate annual return)
- [ ] Deadline: **30 April** (badan). (OP would be 31 March — this is a PT, so 30 April.)
- [ ] The year's monthly **PP55 0.5%** payments (NTPN) pre-fill the return; confirm all 12
      masa are present and paid.
- [ ] Attach the **financial statements** (FS) for the year.
- [ ] While on UMKM final, corporate income is settled by the monthly 0.5% — but the return
      still reports turnover, assets, and the PP55 reconciliation.
- [ ] Capture the **BPE** (bukti penerimaan elektronik — filing receipt).

## LKPM — investment activity report (OSS, for PMDN/PMA)
- [ ] **Quarterly** report in OSS for investment-licensed companies (PMDN included).
- [ ] Confirm who files it and that quarters aren't missed (a common blind spot for small PTs).

## Watch — exit from the 0.5% facility
- [ ] The 0.5% facility for a **PT is time-limited (≈3 years from registration)** and the
      2026 PP55 revision is **removing it for ordinary PT**. Before it lapses, plan the
      switch to **PPh Badan 22%** (income minus expenses) — which needs proper books,
      depreciation, and monthly **PPh 25** installments. Flag the transition year early.

## Possible regional tax
- [ ] Spa/beauty turnover may attract a **local/regional tax** (Pajak Daerah / PB1) and a
      correct KBLI classification. Verify with the local authority if revenue is reclassified.

## Safety
- Prepare only; filing and e-signature require operator approval.
