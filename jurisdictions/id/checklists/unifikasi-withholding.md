# Checklist — Unifikasi (withholding: PPh 4(2) + PPh 23)

The "unifikasi" billing bundles the company's monthly **withholding** obligations other
than payroll. Two common components for a salon/PT:

## PPh 4(2) — final tax on rent (sewa)
- [ ] If the company rents its premises: **10%** of the monthly rent, **final**.
- [ ] KAP-KJS **411128-403**. Withheld by the tenant (the company) and remitted.

## PPh 23 — withholding on services
- [ ] On qualifying domestic services/royalties: **2%** of the fee (non-PKP counterparties
      without NPWP: higher). KAP-KJS **411124-100**.

## Combine, pay, report
- [ ] One **unifikasi** kode billing in Coretax can carry both KAP-KJS lines for the masa;
      amount = sum of the components above.
- [ ] Pay by the **15th**; file **SPT Masa Unifikasi** by the **20th**; capture NTPN.
- [ ] Issue **bukti potong** (withholding slips) to the counterparties via Coretax.

## Notes
- Rent PPh 4(2) is a fixed monthly line whenever rent is paid — don't drop it in months the
  invoice timing shifts; reconcile to the lease.
- This is **withholding the company remits on others' income** — distinct from the company's
  own PP55 (0.5% on turnover) and PPh 21 (employees).
