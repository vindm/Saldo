# Group Profile — Team

These clients are assigned to us by an outsourcing company; we keep their books on the company's behalf. Defaults for everyone in this group — a client's own `profile.md` overrides any line here.

## Communication
- No direct contact with the client. All client-facing questions go through the company's manager.
- Coordination happens in the company's task manager (Finkoper), not in a chat with the client.

## Roles
- A manager on the company side owns the client relationship and approvals.
- Colleagues on the company team may pick up or hand off tasks.

## Data & connectors
- Tasks and client chats: Finkoper.
- Bank statements usually arrive through the company/manager, not pulled directly.

## Tone
- Internal, concise, professional — addressed to colleagues, not to the client.
